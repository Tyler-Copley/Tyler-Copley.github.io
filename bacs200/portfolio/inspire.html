Keanu Reeves.

Keanu Reeves.

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that has grit and never gives up. Heroes are shinning role models to look up to. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Unyeilding-Don't stay down for long
  • Enable others - help others acheive their goals
  • Humble - does not boastful about success
  • Generous - sees others in need and gives without hesitation
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

He may not be a world changing figure, but I find him to be one of the best role models of today. He has felt numerous hardships including: his father leaving at three, He attended 4 different high schools and struggled with dyslexia, his best friend River Phoenix died of a drug overdose at 23, 1999 Jennifer Syme was pregnant with their daughter, but the child was stillborn and it cost them their relationship, 18 months later, Jennifer was killed in a car accident, His sister had leukemia. He suffered through all this, yet he still tries to help others. He gives a large sum of his wealth to charities, largely those working on leukemia treatment. He lives in a two-bedroom home, instead of the mansion he could afford. And finally he is down to earth, like riding the subway even though he is worth over $100 million. He is a great example of success in the face of overwhelming adversity.
